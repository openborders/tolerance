Idee 1
1. User visits page
2. provides his/her nationality;
3. answers 10 questions to fundamental issues (i.e. related to survival instinct; e.g. your house was bombed by air forces of a neighboring country; you survived your small daughter was sleeping in the bedroom);
4. is presented with the answers of a person of another nationality and is asked to guess the nationality

Expected Outcome:
- People will not be able to distinguish the answers from other countries from their own people.
- People realize, that other nationalities are really not that different
